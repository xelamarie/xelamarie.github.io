Oregon Trail Country Club

Everyone knows that a bad day of golf beats a good day of work – hands down. So why not spend your bad and good days here with us at the Oregon Trail Country Club. Open seven days a week, we have everything to make your golf experience enjoyable. Play right through the ruts left by the wagons on the Oregon Trail! The nine hole, 36 par golf course is open to the public. Golf equipment is available for rent at the clubhouse.

We are just over a month into our summer season and into our weekly BLITZ NIGHTS! If you haven’t played or don’t know what these are; we would like to change that! These are super fun evenings. Come with a partner or come without and be paired with one! It’s a great way to meet new people and have a blast playing one of the greatest games out there.
