Fishing in the Oneida Narrows Reservoir

Oneida Narrows is a beautiful reservoir for fishing, boating, public-lands-campground. Bring the family to to fish for Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, and Yellow Perch. Please use your best judgement when determining where you can fish, and make sure you follow local rules and regulations.

Oneida Narrows has 10 developed campsites that have picnic tables and fire rings. The campground has boat ramps and docks. There is an overnight camping fee. There is not a reservation system for the campsites. This is a first-come, first-served campground that is usually full on weekends. Camp sites must be occupied nightly. No holding sites. 14 day camping limit within a 28 day period. No ATV/UTV riding allowed in campground. Pets must be on leash at all times.
