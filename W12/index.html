Who We Are

Our chamber has your best business interests in mind! We want to help you reach your goals and see measurable success. There is no better way to spread the word about your business than to make connections with others. Whether you're new to our community or have been around for awhile, your business reputation grows when you engage with other businesses around you. We invite you to join with us in our Chamber of Commerce that has been going strong for 63 years now! We aim to see you thrive as a local business. Join us today (link at bottom of page)!

This Week's Company Spotlights

Upcoming Events

Christian Brothers Automotive

Seeking to change the way customers think about auto service, Christian Brothers Automotive promises knowledge, honesty, and genuine car repairs. In business since 1982, they have always lived by their motto of “Love your neighbor as yourself.”

Free Child Car Seat Safety Event
Thu Jul 22nd 12:00pm - 3:00pm

Ever wonder if your child's car seat is installed properly? Does the seat itself meet safety standards? The Meridian Fire Department is hosting a free child safety seat check. Please contact the Meridian Fire Department to schedule an appointment @ 208-888-1234.

Meridian Library District

Books, classes, events, reading programs...You name it, they've got it! The Meridian Library District provides equal access to information that supports a wide variety of community needs. Through their services and resources, the are able to "enrich lives, ignite curiosity, and cultivate connections."

Sparklight Movie Night in Meridian - Tangled
Fri Jul 30th 7:00pm

Meridian Parks & Recreation is pleased to announce the return of Sparklight Movie Night. This event will take place in Meridian’s Settlers Park. Movies are projected onto a 30-foot inflatable screen in a roped off seating area within the park. Blankets and low-backed chairs are encouraged. The movies begin at dusk.

Treasure Valley Children’s Theater

Empowering youth through theater arts is the mission of Treasure Valley Children's Theater. Building confidence, friendships, creativity, and experience is the name of the game at TVCT.

New Art Exhibit & Artist's Reception
Fri Aug 6th 4:30pm - 7:00pm

This new exhibit, located in Meridian’s Initial Point Gallery located on the third floor of Meridian City Hall, features works by Wendy Blickenstaff, Clare Nelson, and Lauri Borer. It will remain on display until Thursday, September 2, 2021.
