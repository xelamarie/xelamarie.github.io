Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat

Montserrat is a sans-serif font that produces clean, readable, and modern headings.

Paragraph Font: Lora

Lora is serif font that complements the modern feel of the page. It contrasts well with the sans-serif Montserrat font and has nice curvature in its serifs.
