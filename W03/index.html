Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to not only provide viewers with local weather updates, but to get them excited about venturing out into the community...rain or shine! Local activities and entertainment will be recommended based on current weather conditions. Rainy in Soda Springs? Head to the Theatre! Clear skies in Preston? Hot Springs, here we come!

In addition to useful information and helpful suggestions, users can also submit their own weather-related pictures for publication in the community gallery. Did a trampoline end up on your roof after the storm? We want to see it! Does the cloud in the sky look like a dinosaur? Please share!
